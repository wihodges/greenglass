About green glass
 
 
green glass was established in 2012 by Julie van Oosten who is passionate about terrariums and the endless creative and aesthetic possibilities they hold. Using a combination of natural and man made materials, Julie has become a collector of embellishments, and has developed a fascination for the diversity of plants, especially succulents and semi tropical plants.
 
green glass terrariums have been purchased to decorate:
doctor and dental waiting rooms
cafe counters and tables.
office spaces.
and of course, many lovely homes.

Julie's studio is located in the Brisbane suburb of Moorooka.
